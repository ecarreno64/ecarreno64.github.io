About This Web App
About the Project

Our interactive web app displays U.S. fuel expenditure data using a choropleth map, showing how fuel-related costs vary across states. It includes information on total expenditures, cost per gallon, and fuel exports. The app is designed for researchers, students, and the general public to explore how essential and economically significant fuel is to the United States.
